Udaipur
The White City


Nestled among the hills of the Aravalli Range in Rajasthan, Udaipur is known for its picturesque lakes, the white colour of its buildings, and the amazing City Palace, constructed with dazzling white marble and gracing the banks of Pichola Lake. Udaipur is also known for its silk carpets, paintings, marble crafts and artistry in silver and terracotta.
